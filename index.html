About Me

Hi there, thanks for visiting my portfolio page! This is Nan, Fang-Ying, aka NaN, Mr. Not A Number!! I’m a Senior Software Engineer and Tech Lead with a focus on building scalable, reliable, and intelligent systems. My background spans full-stack development, cloud infrastructure, and team leadership — delivering impactful products with TypeScript, Python, React, NestJS, and AWS. I thrive in environments that value technical excellence, clear architecture, and continuous improvement through automation and strong engineering practices.

Recently I’ve become deeply invested in AI-driven development — using large language models and automation to make software smarter and more efficient. I’ve built projects like LetterOn, an AI mail assistant that digitizes physical letters using OCR and NLP, and easy-rag, a Retrieval-Augmented Generation CLI that validates responses using LLM-based fuzzy testing. These experiments combine my love for elegant engineering with the creative potential of generative AI.

Outside of coding, I find balance on the slopes and in the cellar. I’m an enthusiastic snowboarder and a WSET Level 3 wine certificate holder, always chasing depth — whether it’s carving clean lines in powder or exploring the complexity behind a good Bordeaux. For me, technology, snow, and wine share the same essence: precision, curiosity, and craft.
